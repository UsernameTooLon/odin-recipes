Vegetarian Lasagne

Description

This is the best vegetarian lasagna recipe you'll ever make. To simplify the prep, you can use a large saute pan with a lid. Saute first the onions for a minute, then add the garlic and mushrooms and saute all of that for another minute or so, then add fresh spinach leaves on top and cover it all with the lid until the spinach is steamed and softened (about 5 minutes).

Feel free to use twice as much spinach, too!
